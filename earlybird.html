INTRODUCTION

Sanem is young girl who hadn't succeeded in landing a permanent job yet, and chooses to work at her father's grocery store in hopes to become a famous writer one day. When her parents give her a choice to either get a proper job or an arranged marriage, Sanem rushes to a job in the advertising agency her sister works in. There her adventure with the two boss, Jan, begins, starting a chain of events that end up with her falling in love with Jan.
